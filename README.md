# AdventureWorks Sales Performance Dashboard

## Snapshot

**Business scenario:** Simulated business intelligence case study using AdventureWorks sales data.

**Business question:** How can a retail business monitor sales performance, identify growth opportunities, and support better commercial decision-making?

**Dashboard focus:** Sales performance, product trends, customer behaviour, regional performance, and management-level KPIs.

**Tools:** Power BI, DAX, Power Query, Excel

**Methods:** Data modelling, KPI design, DAX measures, interactive dashboarding, sales trend analysis, product analysis, customer analysis, and regional performance reporting

**Output:** Interactive Power BI dashboard, dashboard screenshots, and business intelligence case study README

---

## About This Project

This project is based on a simulated business intelligence brief using AdventureWorks sales data.

AdventureWorks is treated as a fictional retail business that needs better visibility over sales performance. The goal was to design a Power BI dashboard that helps managers monitor revenue, profit, order trends, product performance, customer behaviour, and regional sales patterns.

The purpose of this project is to demonstrate how Power BI can be used to turn sales data into a clear management reporting tool for business users.

---

## Business Scenario

AdventureWorks has sales data across products, customers, dates, and regions, but business users need a simpler way to understand performance.

The simulated stakeholder brief was to build an interactive dashboard that could help management answer:

* How are revenue, profit, and order volume performing?
* Which products and categories are driving sales?
* Which customer groups contribute most to performance?
* Which regions or markets are performing strongest?
* Are there underperforming products, categories, or areas that need attention?
* How can decision-makers monitor sales performance quickly through interactive KPIs?

The dashboard was designed to make these insights accessible through visuals, slicers, and summary metrics.

## Key Dashboard Features

### 1. Executive KPI View

The dashboard provides high-level KPIs so business users can quickly understand overall performance.

Typical KPI areas include:

* Total sales
* Profit
* Orders
* Quantity sold
* Average order value
* Year-to-date or period-based performance

### 2. Sales Trend Analysis

Time-based visuals help identify changes in sales performance over time.

This supports questions such as:

* Are sales increasing or declining?
* Which periods performed strongest?
* Are there seasonal patterns?
* Where should management investigate changes in performance?

### 3. Product Performance Analysis

The dashboard helps compare product and category performance.

This supports questions such as:

* Which products generate the most revenue?
* Which categories are strongest?
* Are there products with weak performance?
* Which product areas should be prioritised?

### 4. Customer Insight

Customer-level visuals help identify which customers or customer groups contribute most to sales.

This supports questions such as:

* Which customers are most valuable?
* Are sales concentrated among a small group of customers?
* Which customer groups may need retention or growth focus?

### 5. Regional Performance

Regional or territory-based reporting helps compare performance across markets.

This supports questions such as:

* Which regions perform best?
* Which markets are underperforming?
* Where could sales activity or commercial focus be improved?

## Tools, Methods, and Analytical Approach

### Power BI

Used to build the interactive dashboard, create visuals, apply filters, and design the final reporting experience.

### Power Query

Used for data preparation, transformation, and shaping before dashboard development.

### Data Modelling

The data model connects sales, product, customer, geography, and date-related tables to support interactive analysis.

### DAX

Used to create business measures and KPIs for dashboard reporting.

Examples of measure areas include:

* Total sales
* Total profit
* Order count
* Quantity sold
* Average order value
* Time-based performance measures

### Dashboard Design

The dashboard was designed around business questions rather than visuals alone. The focus was to create a clear reporting tool that supports fast interpretation and decision-making.
